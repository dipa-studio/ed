Privacy Policy — Edzésnapló

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Short version: Edzésnapló does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Data that stays on your device

Edzésnapló is an offline workout tracker. All the data you enter — workout logs, notes, dates — is stored locally on your device using an on-device database (Room/SQLite). This data is never sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

2. Data we do not collect

Edzésnapló does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data from sensors or other apps
  • Device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data sent to us or any third party

3. Network access

Edzésnapló does not require an internet connection to function. The app does not make any network requests for analytics, ads, tracking, or syncing.

4. Third-party services

Edzésnapló contains no third-party SDKs, no advertisements, and no analytics. There are no embedded web views or external content loaders.

5. Backups and data export

If you back up your device through Android's built-in backup features, your workout logs may be included in that local backup. We have no access to those backups. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device.

6. Children's privacy

Edzésnapló is suitable for users aged 13 and over. Since no data is collected from any user, no special provisions for children are needed.

7. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach the developer at the email address listed on the Google Play listing for this app.
